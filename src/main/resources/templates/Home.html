Lost2Found: Your Smart Lost & Found Solution

Lost2Found is a digital platform designed to address the common challenge of misplacing belongings in various environments. Traditional methods like notice boards, registers, or word-of-mouth are often slow and inefficient. Our platform provides a modern, streamlined solution for reporting, tracking, and recovering lost or found items.

Digital Reporting

Users can easily report lost or found items with complete details like title, description, location, date, and optional images.

AI-Powered Matching

Our intelligent AI engine automatically suggests potential matches between reports, minimizing human effort and maximizing accuracy.

Enhanced Trust & Transparency

The platform is designed to be secure and reliable, with an admin verification module that ensures system integrity and reduces false claims.

Improved Recovery

By providing a secure, structured, and user-friendly platform, we significantly enhance the chances of item recovery.
